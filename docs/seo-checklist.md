# SEO Verification Checklist

## Metadata

- Canonical URLs resolve correctly
- Open Graph title and description render
- Twitter card metadata renders
- Homepage, category, tool, and landing pages have meaningful titles and descriptions

## Structured data

- Tool pages emit `SoftwareApplication`
- Category pages emit `CollectionPage`
- SEO landing pages emit `CollectionPage` with `ItemList`

## Crawlability

- `/robots.txt` is live
- `/sitemap.xml` is live
- Tool URLs use stable slugs
- Category URLs use stable slugs
- SEO landing pages are listed in the sitemap

## Rendering

- Tool detail pages render server-side
- Category pages render server-side
- SEO landing pages build successfully

## Content quality

- Tool titles and taglines are concise and descriptive
- Descriptions are unique
- Category descriptions are unique
- Featured and popular tools are visible above the fold where relevant
